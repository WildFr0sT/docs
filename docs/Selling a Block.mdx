# Selling a Block

The instance you create a block using the CLI, it will appear in the store as a private and other pages of Appblocks in your logged in account.  

There are three ways you can sell your block:
1. Through **Manage Blocks** (hyperlink)
    * In the table, blocks with license as “Own” are the blocks created by you.  
    * There will be a call to action to sell the block.  
    * On clicking the Sell Block, you will be redirected to fill a form.
   
2. Through **Sales History** (hyperlink)  
    * You will find two tables with different information - Sales information, Private blocks.  
    * Sales information will list details of your blocks that are already on sale, and you will have the option of delisting that block.  
    * On this page, you will see an option to sell a block.  
    * Takes you to a form where you will choose the block you want to list.  
    * Private blocks will be your list of blocks that were delisted/never on sale.  
    * In this table, you will see an option to sell the block against each.  
    * Clicking on the action will take you to a form to fill out the details.
 3, Through store

* Submitting the form will let you publish the block. Now your block will be on sale.

### Delisting your block
* To delist your block or remove it from sale, go to 
* Manage Blocks > Table > Delist.
* Sales History > Sales Information table > Delist
