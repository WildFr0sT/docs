# Buying a Block

1.  Login to your Appblocks account and visit the store at https://store.appblocks.com/  

3.  In the store there are blocks put on sale. You can use the filters, sorts and search to find blocks of your liking.  
 
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/33730398/203917234-f0745a58-36d4-46df-b791-9176ad260fe9.png)  

3. Clicking on each block can show you its details.  

![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/33730398/203917373-cd7979c3-0d0a-4ed9-9b9e-da4fadc6cb24.png)  

4. In the details page, choose the kind of **license and subscription** (hyperlink) you want for the block. Following this, you may choose the space you want to buy the block into, and add it to your cart.  
  
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/33730398/203917836-bb9de82b-4ea3-4677-ba8b-7325a7237f01.png)  

5. Once you add it to your cart, you will be taken to the cart page.  
 
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/33730398/203918159-523c243b-3cae-4e69-90bd-f2f9f39fea1d.png)  

7. Read and accept the terms and conditions.  

9. Secure Checkout.
