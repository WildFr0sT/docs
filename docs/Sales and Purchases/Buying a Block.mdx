# Buying a Block

Blocks can be free or paid. When you go through the store and find a block or your liking, click it for more details. 
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/33730398/217485953-8268dcca-3a33-4d7f-bbf5-0ebe78523b62.png) 
(ss has to be changed because of 0$ and blocks without paid/free mentioning)

#### Free block  
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/33730398/217484496-6eef0402-ccb7-4b46-a5d2-5a5a6148ac19.png)
1. Copy paste the command in bb-cli (read instructions for installation in another doc)

#### Paid block
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/33730398/217485241-19ccdaee-1e0b-46e8-8655-dc671a5fa6e9.png)  

1. To purchase a paid block, you should be a logged in user. So login to your Appblocks account. 

2. In the details page, choose the kind of [license and subscription](https://docs.appblocks.com/docs/Licences%20and%20Subscriptions) you want for the block. Following this, you may choose the [space](https://docs.appblocks.com/docs/Spaces) you want to buy the block into, and add it to your cart.  
  
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/33730398/217492383-e0b84bbf-63d8-45f5-bf50-d8ea75103868.png)

3. Once you add it to your cart, you will be taken to the cart page.  Read and accept the terms and conditions.
 
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/33730398/217497087-b35dafb3-c025-4e34-86af-81828c068f76.png)

4. Secure Checkout with Stripe and your order will be placed.
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/33730398/203919780-822d1c50-d446-4306-b2bd-a86604af87ad.png)  

>[How to use your purchased block and see it in action.](https://docs.appblocks.com/docs/Getting%20Started%20with%20blocks#using-the-purchased-block)
