# Tracking your Sales, Orders and Block Management

## Sales History
Inside Spaces ([Read more](https://docs.appblocks.com/docs/Spaces)), on the side menu, you will find Sales History. Within Sales History, you can do the following:
* Find sales information
* Filter out information specific to a Space
* Sell a Block
* Delist a block
* Find your blocks that are not listed currently
* Manage all your blocks

## Manage Blocks
Inside Spaces, on the side menu, you will find Manage Blocks. Within Manage Blocks, you can do the following:
* Find information on all blocks associated with your account (blocks created by you, listed/on sale blocks, purchased blocks, etc)
* Filter out Blocks information specific to a Space
* Assign app to a block you bought
* Reassign app if the block assigned to an app has not been used
* Install code for a purchased block
* Update listing for blocks if you have the permission **(confirm)**
* Renew Subscriptions ([Read more](https://docs.appblocks.com/docs/Licences%20and%20Subscriptions))
* **Cancel Auto-Renewals?**
